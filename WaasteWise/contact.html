Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Simply visit our homepage, select your category (Farmer, Industrialist, or Citizen), and fill out the waste assessment form. You'll receive personalized recommendations immediately!

Is my data private?

Yes, all data is processed locally in your browser. We don't store any of your personal information on our servers.

Can I use WasteWise on mobile?

Absolutely! WasteWise is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

What should I do with my waste?

Our platform provides specific recommendations based on your waste type. Follow the suggestions in the results page for each module.

Do you charge for using WasteWise?

No! WasteWise is completely free to use. We believe waste management should be accessible to everyone.

Can businesses use WasteWise?

Yes! Our Industrialist module is specifically designed for businesses and factories to track and optimize their waste management.
