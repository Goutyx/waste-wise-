Ways to Reduce Your Waste

Proper Waste Segregation

Separate your waste into categories for proper disposal and recycling.

  • Use separate bins for wet waste, dry waste, and plastic
  • Follow your city's waste management guidelines
  • Reduces environmental impact by up to 50%

Composting Wet Waste

Convert food scraps into nutrient-rich compost for your garden.

  • Start a small home composting bin
  • Use compost for plants and gardens
  • Reduces landfill waste by 30-40%

Recycling Dry Waste

Recycle paper, cardboard, and other dry materials.

  • Collect dry waste for recycling centers
  • Get paid for recyclable materials
  • Supports circular economy initiatives

Reduce Plastic Usage

Minimize plastic consumption and switch to sustainable alternatives.

  • Use reusable bags, bottles, and containers
  • Buy products with minimal plastic packaging
  • Target: 50% reduction in plastic waste

Smart Shopping Habits

Make conscious choices while shopping to reduce waste.

  • Buy in bulk to reduce packaging waste
  • Choose products with eco-friendly packaging
  • Avoid single-use items

Community Initiatives

Join community waste management programs.

  • Participate in local cleanup drives
  • Share waste reduction tips with neighbors
  • Support community composting programs

Quick Eco Tips

Plan meals to reduce food waste and save money

Reuse containers for storage instead of buying new ones

Donate used items instead of throwing them away

Use digital alternatives to reduce paper consumption
