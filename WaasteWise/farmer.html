Recommendations to Reduce Waste

Composting Program

Convert your crop residue and animal waste into nutrient-rich compost.

  • Create compost pits for organic waste decomposition
  • Use compost to improve soil health and reduce fertilizer costs
  • Estimated benefit: 40-50% reduction in chemical fertilizer use

Biogas Generation

Use animal waste to generate biogas for cooking and electricity.

  • Install biogas digesters for waste conversion
  • Generate clean energy while managing waste

Crop Rotation & Mulching

Manage crop residue effectively through rotation and mulching practices.

  • Use crop residue as mulch to retain soil moisture
  • Practice crop rotation to reduce pest buildup
  • Reduces need for chemical pesticides by 30%

Vermiculture

Use earthworms to convert organic waste into vermicompost.

  • Faster decomposition compared to traditional composting
  • High-quality fertilizer for organic farming
  • Additional income source by selling vermicompost

Sustainable Practices

Adopt eco-friendly farming methods to minimize waste generation.

  • Reduce burning of crop residue to minimize air pollution
  • Use drip irrigation to conserve water
  • Adopt precision farming techniques
