## How to Use

### Getting Started

1. **Open the Website**
   - Simply open `index.html` in any modern web browser
   - No installation or server setup required

2. **Navigate to Your Module**
   - Click on your category: Farmer, Industrialist, or Citizen
   - Each module has a dedicated page with a form

### Farmer Module

1. Fill in your farm details:
   - Full name and location
   - Contact number
   - Type of farming
   - Farm area
   - Daily crop residue
   - Daily animal waste

2. Click "Calculate Waste & Get Suggestions"

3. View your results:
   - Daily, monthly, and yearly waste totals
   - Biogas production potential
   - Personalized recommendations

### Industrialist Module

1. Enter your company information:
   - Company name
   - Industry type
   - Daily production units
   - Waste type
   - Daily waste quantity

2. Click "Generate Waste Report"

3. Analyze your metrics:
   - Daily, monthly, yearly waste
   - Waste per unit produced
   - Weekly distribution analysis
   - Strategic recommendations

### Citizen Module

1. Provide your household information:
   - Name and city
   - Family size
   - House type
   - Daily waste by category (wet, dry, plastic)

2. Click "Analyze My Waste"

3. Review your analysis:
   - Total waste generation
   - Waste breakdown by type
   - Per-person waste calculation
   - Actionable reduction strategies
